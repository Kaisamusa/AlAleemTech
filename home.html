Who are we?

We are Al-Aleem Tech Foundation. A non-for profit, and NGO organization that was established in April of 2021.

The foundation is a Liberian based foundation and located in Montserrado, Monrovia, Liberia

Vision and Mission

Our goal is to expose high school students, and graduates to various career opportunities in the field of Technology

Our Vision is to make Liberians marketable, and valuable in the field of Technology. So, that some days, we ourselves can contribute positively to our economy.

Services

We provide Tech support to Schools, Businesses, Organizations and individuals.

Some of our services include:

1.Tech Training

2.Building and managing website

3.Creating Logo and flyers

4.Printing T. Shirts
